Welcome to Our Lipbalm Project

Welcome to our IB Group 4 Science Project! We have created a natural lipbalm using carefully selected ingredients that provide maximum moisturization and protection for your lips. About Our Project

This project explores the chemistry and benefits of natural lipbalm ingredients. Our Natural Ingredients

ü•• Shea Butter

Scientific Name: Vitellaria paradoxa

Shea butter is a fat extracted from the nut of the African shea tree. It's rich in fatty acids and vitamins, making it an excellent natural moisturizer.

Benefits:

  • Deep Moisturization: Contains oleic, stearic, palmitic, and linolenic acids that deeply hydrate lips
  • Anti-inflammatory Properties: Helps soothe chapped and irritated lips
  • Rich in Vitamins: Vitamins A and E promote cell regeneration and healing
  • Natural UV Protection: Provides mild sun protection (SPF 3-4)
  • Long-lasting Protection: Creates a protective barrier without clogging pores
  • Anti-aging Properties: Boosts collagen production for smoother lips

üêù Beeswax

Scientific Name: Cera alba

Beeswax is a natural wax produced by honey bees. It serves as a natural emulsifier and helps create the perfect consistency for lipbalm.

Benefits:

  • Protective Barrier: Forms a protective layer on lips while allowing them to breathe
  • Locks in Moisture: Seals in hydration without drying out lips
  • Anti-bacterial Properties: Natural antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties
  • Rich in Vitamin A: Supports cell reconstruction and health
  • Perfect Texture: Provides ideal consistency and structure to the lipbalm
  • Long-lasting Formula: Helps lipbalm stay on lips longer
  • Natural Preservative: Extends the shelf life of the product
